Dark Mode

Dark mode, sometimes called dark theme or night mode, is a display setting that changes the color scheme of your user interface from the traditional light background with dark text to a dark background with light text. Here's what you need to know about it:

Appearance

  • Background: Switches from white or bright shades to black, dark grey, or other deep colors.
  • Text and elements: Changes from black or dark shades to white, light grey, or other bright colors.

Benefits:

  • Reduced eye strain: Especially in low-light environments, dark mode can be easier on your eyes as it emits less glare and brightness.
  • Improved sleep: By reducing blue light exposure, dark mode might help you fall asleep quicker and sleep better.
  • Battery life: On OLED screens, dark mode can actually improve battery life as fewer pixels are actively lit.
  • Aesthetics: Many people find dark mode visually appealing and create a more immersive viewing experience.
